## üß† UX PRINCIPLES TO APPLY

* **Consistency across platforms**, but leverage platform-native behavior
* **Progressive Disclosure**: Only show filters, categories, etc. when tapped on mobile
* **Touch-first on mobile**: Button hit areas, swipeable components
* **Accessibility**: Contrast ratios, label all icons with `aria-label`
